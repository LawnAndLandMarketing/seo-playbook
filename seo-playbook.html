Google Business Profile

Local presence, automated at scale

Onboarding — One Time

Profile Build & Optimization

  • Complete profile to 100% — every field filled
  • All services added with descriptions
  • Service areas configured accurately
  • Photos uploaded (exterior, work, team)
  • Business info verified — hours, phone, website, address
Ongoing — Monthly

Posts, Reviews & Optimization

  • 52 weekly posts scheduled one year in advance
  • Seasonal, promotional, and educational mix
  • Automated review replies via Zapier + Claude AI
  • Ongoing GBP audits — categories, attributes, Q&A
🌐

Website

Built to rank, built to convert

Build — One Time

Page Structure & Content

  • Core service pages (Lawn Care, Landscaping, Hardscaping, etc.)
  • Sub-service pages for every individual service
  • Service area pages — city and region-specific landing pages
  • All content keyword-targeted and locally relevant
Ongoing

Maintenance & Internal Linking

  • Internal linking between pages as content grows
  • Page updates when services or service areas change
  • Alt text on all new images
⚠️
Known Gap: Alt text audit on existing pages is in progress. All new content is covered.
⚙️

Technical SEO

The foundation everything else is built on

Onboarding — One Time

Technical Setup

  • Rank Math setup and configuration on WordPress
  • Schema markup — LocalBusiness, Service, FAQ, Breadcrumb
  • XML sitemap submitted to Google Search Console
  • robots.txt review and optimization
  • 301 redirect audit
Ongoing

Monitoring & Maintenance

  • Schema updates when services or business info changes
  • Crawl error and indexing issue resolution via GSC
  • Technical issue response as surfaced in monthly GSC review
⚠️
Known Gap: Rank Math configuration is not yet fully standardized across all clients. Standardization is a current priority.
📊

Google Search Console

Ranking data, indexing health, content opportunities

Onboarding — One Time

Account Setup

  • GSC account setup and property verification
  • Domain vs. URL prefix configuration
  • Sitemap submission
Ongoing — Monthly

Performance Review

  • Monthly review — impressions, clicks, average position
  • Indexing issue monitoring and resolution
  • Keyword opportunity identification (positions 4–20)
⚠️
Known Gap: GSC monitoring is not yet on a standardized reporting cadence. Formalizing this is a current priority.
✍️

Content

Fresh signals, every month

Monthly

Blog & On-Page Content

  • 1 blog post per client — locally relevant, keyword-optimized
  • Alt text on all images in new posts and pages
  • Internal links added to and from every new piece of content
⚠️
Known Gap: 1 blog/month is the current baseline — not the ceiling. Increasing content cadence is a near-term priority.
🔗

Link Building & Citations

Authority built over time

Onboarding — One Time

Citation Foundation

  • Citation submission via Whitespark — top 50+ directories
  • NAP consistency audit and directory cleanup
Ongoing — 2x/Month

Press Releases

  • 2 press releases per month via Signal Genesis / Search Atlas
  • PR #1 links to homepage
  • PR #2 links to current month's blog post
⚠️
Known Gap: No ongoing citation building or backlink outreach beyond press releases. A repeatable system for this is actively being developed.
